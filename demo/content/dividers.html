Divider

<au-divider> is a thin line that separates content into clear groups. Supports horizontal and vertical orientations.

When to use

Use dividers to separate content into logical groups. They're ideal for lists, toolbars, and card sections. Add the inset attribute when dividing items with leading icons to align with the content.
